## 2026-03-01 - [Contrast Improvement]
**Learning:** The default `COL_DIM` (#646669) on the background #323437 has a contrast ratio of ~2.15:1, which is below the WCAG AA recommendation of 4.5:1 for normal text.
**Action:** Always check contrast ratios for secondary text and hints to ensure they meet accessibility standards while maintaining the intended visual hierarchy.

## 2026-03-01 - [History Summary Stats]
**Learning:** Adding aggregate statistics (like total tests and average WPM) to a history view provides immediate value to the user without having to scan the entire table. It gives a quick sense of progress and overall performance.
**Action:** Include summary or aggregate data in list/table views to improve the at-a-glance usefulness of the interface.
